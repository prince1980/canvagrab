How Canva Portfolio Videos Work

Canva portfolio pages (typically hosted at yourname.my.canva.site) let creators showcase their work with embedded video backgrounds and video sections. These videos play inline on the page but are delivered using DASH adaptive streaming, which splits audio and video into separate network requests.

This makes it impossible to download them with a simple right-click. That's where CanvaGrab comes in.

How CanvaGrab Downloads Portfolio Videos

  1. A headless Firefox browser opens your Canva portfolio URL
  2. It scrolls through the entire page to trigger lazy-loaded video content
  3. Every .mp4 and .m4a network request is intercepted and captured
  4. Video and audio streams are paired and merged using FFmpeg
  5. Complete MP4 files are served for instant download

Works on All Canva Portfolio Formats

  • ✅ Canva portfolio sites — yourname.my.canva.site
  • ✅ Custom domain portfolios — Canva pages published to your own domain
  • ✅ Multi-page portfolios — paste each page URL to download its videos
  • ✅ Background videos — hero sections with video backgrounds are captured too
